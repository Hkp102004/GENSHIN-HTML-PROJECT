VENTI

Venti is the anemo archon who controls the wind and knows as the ruler of mondstadth although he's a really kind and gentle archon who never ruled the people but instead let them be comepletly free and enjoy the freedom. The people of mondstadth still never forget to thank him for his kindness and the freedom he gave them and pray to him as lord barbatos. Venti is said to be really powerfull during old times, he can make black holes, split mountails. He can control the wind but nowadays he just disguises himself as a plain bard to live among normal people.

ZHONGLI

Zhongli is the geo archon who controls the earth and knows as the ruler of Liyue, the oldest archon of all, he is the one who made mora(the currency of teyvat). He posseses geo elemental power and is resposible for the ditribution of the geo vision. His god name is Morax and everyone in teyvat knows him only by this name. Morax is said to be most powerfull of all archons because he is also a geo dragon sovereign. He fought many battles and is said that the islands near liyue created because of the polearm he threw at his opponents. He is alos very kind and gentle man who posseses a lot of knowledge and really wise. He faked his own death so he could live among citizens of liyue and now goes by the name Zhongli.

RAIDEN SHOGUN

Raiden is the electro archon who is the ruler of Inazuma, she is rather a cold archon who puts a lot of restrictions on her people to achieve eternity. Her goal is to achieve eternity which she promisted to her people. She is very powerful and holds almost same power level as Zhongli. She is known for her power, she fought many wars and defeated several enemies with just one strike. She stored her real self inside a sword and lives in the plane of euthaimiya. She is also known as baal and is said to be most beautifull in all over teyvat and now she lives in plane of euthaimiya and the robot Raiden Shogun acts as her and does all her duty. Raiden's real name is Ei.
